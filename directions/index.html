Directions

The workshop will take place in Firenze Fiera, Florence's best exhibition and conference venue, situated in the city centre, just a short walk from the main train station. Firenze Fiera is well-connected by public transportation, including trains, trams, buses, and taxis. Additionally, a large parking facility is available beneath the nearby railway station.

The workshop room is in Palazzo dei Congressi, which is located within the 19th-century Villa Vittoria. Nestled in a historic park, it radiates timeless elegance while featuring cutting-edge technology. It offers versatile spaces for various conferences and events, including meeting rooms, a 1,000-seat auditorium, and a modern amphitheatre surrounded by greenery. This makes it one of the most distinguished and captivating venues in Florence.
